​Skin Dance is your best Spa choice in Santa Barbara. 
At Skin Dance Spa we offer a variety of exquisite spa facials, massages and holistic therapies featuring the finest natural and organic skin care products by Jurlique, combined with treatments developed over 30 years by
​Skin Dance founder, Andra White.

NOTOX! We are not a medi-spa but we find that our treatments work very well alongside medi-spa procedures. In some cases, our skin care techniques, superior products, and a life-time's experience may have an impact on the healing process, such as reducing healing times,

24 years in Santa Barbara!  Skin Dance first opened in 1995 in a small space in Santa Barbara, and has been rejuvenating faces, bodies, and spirits ever since. Skin Dance Spa is a serene space offering a warm and inviting ambience as well as a beautiful ﻿retail boutique﻿ featuring the finest skincare products, essential oils, incense, candles, and so much more.

Skin Dance Spa has attracted an excellent team of some of the best estheticians, massage therapists and healers in the Santa Barbara area whose soul intention is to take care of all that your skin, body and spirit needs. Stop by and find out why we are among the very best day spas in
​Santa Barbara!
