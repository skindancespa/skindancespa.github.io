Beauty. Balance. Bliss.
Ayurveda Immersion for Women
October 28th - November 18th
If you're curious about Ayurveda and how it could benefit your life, this is the perfect course for you!
If you are struggling with anxiety, indigestion, hot flashes and other uncomfortable symptoms in your body, take this course to become highly skilled in dealing with these and other life challenges.

This 4 week course meets every Monday from October 28 - November 18th, 6:30 - 7:30 pm at Skin Dance.
Our in-house Ayurvedic Specialist, Aparna Khanolkar, will guide you through an immersion into Ayurveda, systematically teaching you practical ways to apply Ayurveda into your daily life. You then go home and have the immediate experience of integrating this ancient art of living to bring your body and mind back to their most natural balanced state. You will learn the basics of Ayurveda and the doshas, lifestyle choices, food as medicine, meditation, body care and emotional freedom.

At Skin Dance we want a healthy body, a happy mind and an open, loving heart for every one of our clients. We are thrilled to offer Beauty. Balance. Bliss. and hope you will join us in this life changing course.
