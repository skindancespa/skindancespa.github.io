Citrus Essential Oils & Roll-Ons

Enfleurage Essential Oils
With Enfleurage essential oils all you get is the aromatic soul of the plant. Purely. Nothing is added - no preservatives, no isolates, no “enhancers”, and no alcohol.

All citrus have some actions in common. With only a couple of exceptions, citrus oil is always pressed from the peel, not distilled. All citruses have an uplifting, happy feel and all of them are anti-microbial to some extent.

Citrus prefers to be kept in dark, cool places and will go off after some time, depending largely on how much air finds its way in to oxidize the oil. Citrus oil past its prime will smell insipid, too sweet, and flat, like a forced smile. Throw it away, don't use it! Spoiled citrus oil has actually been known to do the opposite of fresh citrus oil. Also, be careful putting any citrus oil directly on the skin or adding drops directly to the bath without first diluting as it may burn you.  Dilute and you'll be fine!
Orange Sweet  -  Citrus aurantium Dulcis
10ml
Oil of orange has long been known to be anti-depressant and good
for conditions ranging from insomnia to biliousness. Sweet orange
has more of a sunny, sweet and happy note than bitter orange.

Sweet orange oil is great to put in a room diffuser in a child’s room,
as children usually love it.

Sweet orange is also known to be lymph decongestant, as well as a digestive, helping constipation, flatulence and irritable bowel.

Blends well with geranium, lavender, neroli, petigrain, petigrain mandarin.
Bergamot  -  Citrus bergamia                          
10ml      
Pressed from the rind of Italian bergamot fruit. Uplifting is how we best describe bergamot, but this oil doesn't stop there--bergamot has been used as a great antiseptic and is also indicated for digestive conditions.

For skin care, bergamot is a good choice for oily skin and acne, eczema and psoriasis. However, don’t go out in the sun with bergamot on your skin. You will be burned!

Blends well with cistus, jasmine grandiflorum, lavender, rose otto, sandalwood, tea tree. What a versatile oil!                                

Grapefruit Peel - Citrus paradisi
10ml
This grapefruit sings the way grapefruit is supposed to. It comes out of the bottle like a genie! The oil is whole - it has not been robbed of its nooketone. If you don't love grapefruit, try this one for a new experience!

Grapefruit is just good! It's delicious and the smell is thrilling, sparkling! It is an uplifting, stimulating, happy essential oil that is great for any blend for stress, anxiety, nervous exhaustion or depression. Also good in blends for the lymphatic system, the liver and gall bladder. 
For skin care, use it in cleansers, tonics, and lotions for oily skin in particular.

Grapefruit blends well with ginger, peppermint, rosemary, spearmint, vanilla, and ylang ylang.
